The Vanishing Voices

Linguistic diversity in Africa is currently facing an unprecedented decline. Of the roughly 2,000 languages on the continent, over 400 are classified as endangered. This data highlights the "Critical Risk" category—languages with fewer than 1,000 speakers remaining—illustrating the urgent need for the advocacy proposed in this project.
